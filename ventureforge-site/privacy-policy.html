Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026  ·  VentureForge, New Zealand

The short version: We collect only what we need to run your Forge engagement and communicate with you. We don't sell your data, we don't share it with third parties for marketing purposes, and we keep it secure. The details are below.

1. Who We Are

VentureForge is a commercial opportunity discovery and validation service operated by Brendan Ralph, based in New Zealand. Our website is ventureforge.co.nz and you can contact us at hello@ventureforge.co.nz.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and, where applicable, the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

2. Information We Collect

Information you give us directly

When you submit a form on our website — either to apply for a Free Forge, submit a paid Forge brief, or contact us — we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name
  • A description of your business challenge or opportunity
  • Your selected Forge option (Instant, Fast, or Full Forge)
  • Your industry or sector

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website we may automatically collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Your approximate geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referring website or search term

This information is collected via Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity. Neither tool collects personally identifiable information without your consent.

Information from your Forge engagement

If you proceed with a paid Forge engagement, we may collect additional business information you share with us — including company data, market context, competitive information, and strategic priorities. This information is used solely to deliver your Forge report and is treated as confidential.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review and respond to your Forge application or brief
  • Deliver your Forge engagement and report
  • Send you updates relevant to your engagement
  • Improve our methodology and service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails, sell your data to third parties, or share your business information with other clients.

4. Free Forge Reports and Case Studies

If your business is selected for a Free Forge, the resulting report may be published publicly as a case study on our website. We will always obtain your explicit written consent before publishing any report or identifying information about your business. You retain full commercial rights to the insights in your report.

If you prefer to receive the report privately without publication, this can be agreed in advance of the engagement.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your form submissions are stored securely by Netlify (our website host), which is SOC 2 Type II certified. Engagement data and correspondence is stored in our secure email and file management systems with access restricted to VentureForge personnel only.

We retain your information for as long as is necessary to deliver your engagement and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes — typically no longer than three years following your last engagement with us.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our website and deliver our services (Netlify, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity) — these parties are bound by their own privacy policies and only process data as necessary
  • Legal authorities where required by law

8. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information (subject to our legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your information at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner (privacy.org.nz) if you believe your privacy rights have been breached

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@ventureforge.co.nz. We will respond within 20 working days.

9. International Data Transfers

VentureForge is based in New Zealand and primarily serves clients in New Zealand and Australia. Our service providers (including Google and Microsoft) may process data outside New Zealand. We only use providers that meet appropriate data protection standards.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
